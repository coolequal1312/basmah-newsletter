What does BASMAH stand for?
BASMAH is
for the people,
by the people.
It is dedicated to building a happier and more connected workplace by fostering a diverse, inclusive, and supportive environment. The program focuses on enhancing communication, increasing employee engagement, sustaining a healthy work environment, and building team spirit through employee-driven initiatives, real-time feedback, and strategically planned activities. With measurable outcomes and a balance of fun and purpose, BASMAH aims to create a sense of belonging and meaningful connection across the organization.

Introducing Our New Vision
FY26 begins the 5th chapter for BASMAH as we proudly introduce our expanded model, now built on Six Pillars that shape engagement, culture, wellbeing, recognition, talent pipeline, and meaningful connection across SYSS MEA. These pillars reflect who we are as a community and the values we champion every day: communication, inclusion, wellness, growth, appreciation, and connection.
COMMUNICATION
Open, transparent communication that keeps our teams informed, connected, and aligned. Through structured forums and regular updates, this pillar ensures every voice is heard and employees stay engaged with leadership and business priorities.


CULTURE AND INCLUSION
This pillar strengthens team spirit by celebrating diversity, belonging, and cultural identity. From festivals to shared workplace moments, it creates an inclusive environment where everyone feels represented and valued.

BASMAH
CONNECT
Built on connection and community, this pillar brings employees together through conversations, shared experiences, and meaningful dialogue. It nurtures professional growth, peer learning, and strong support networks across teams.
TALENT DEVELOPMENT
This pillar drives growth by building a strong pool of skilled talent through exposure to learning opportunities, leadership mentoring and feedback, peer-to-peer knowledge sharing. Creating a pipeline through regular engagements to ensure readiness of talent.
REWARDS AND RECOGNITION
A pillar dedicated to celebrating effort, achievement, and teamwork. From annual awards to gratitude messages and appreciation events, it reinforces a culture where contributions are valued and success is shared.


HEALTH AND WELLBEING
Focused on mental, and emotional wellness, this pillar promotes mindful breaks, health awareness, activity challenges, and initiatives that help everyone feel supported, energized, and cared for.




This transformation is made possible by the BASMAH Committee. A group of passionate volunteers who drive engagement across the business with creativity, care, and commitment.

Basmah Connect
Monthly Mentorship Sessions
Introduced in January 2026, Basmah Connect encourages knowledge sharing and strengthens connections across teams. The initiative provides a space where colleagues can learn directly from one another’s experience, creating opportunities for open dialogue, peer learning, and professional growth. By inviting leaders and specialists from different areas of the organization to host sessions, the program helps employees stay informed, broaden their perspectives, and build stronger networks within the workplace.

The series began with Bassem Alsharif’s session on “Breaking the Blame Game,” which focused on fostering accountability and constructive problem-solving within teams. This was followed by Kanika Vaish’s session on Career Development & Progression, where employees gained insights into navigating career growth and making intentional development choices. The most recent session by Lito Ramos introduced the fundamentals of Power BI, demonstrating how it can be used as a practical tool to turn data into meaningful insights and support better decision-making.

These first sessions set the foundation for a year-long learning series aimed at strengthening collaboration and continuous development across the organization.
